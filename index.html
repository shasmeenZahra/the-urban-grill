Our Story

Two brothers, one grill, zero shortcuts

Founded in 2021 by two chef brothers, The Urban Grill grew out of childhood memories of Sunday BBQ in the backyard. We left five-star hotel kitchens to build something simpler: a place where quality meets comfort.

Today we feed 300+ guests a day, and we run on one rule — if we won't eat it, we won't serve it.

"If we won't eat it, we won't serve it."
